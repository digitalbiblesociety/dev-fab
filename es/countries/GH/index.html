Languages Maps Language Maps
Ghana a nation on West Africa’s Gulf of Guinea is known for diverse wildlife old forts and secluded beaches such as at Busua. Coastal towns Elmina and Cape Coast contain posubans (native shrines) colonial buildings and castles-turned-museums that serve as testimonials to the slave trade. North of Cape Coast vast Kakum National Park has a treetop-canopy walkway over the rainforest. Wikipedia
