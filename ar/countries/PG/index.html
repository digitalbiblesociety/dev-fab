Papua New Guinea in the southwestern Pacific encompasses the eastern half of New Guinea and its offshore islands. A country of immense cultural and biological diversity it’s known for its beaches and coral reefs. Inland are active volcanoes granite Mt. Wilhelm dense rainforest and hiking routes like the Kokoda Trail. There are also traditional tribal villages many with their own languages. Wikipedia
